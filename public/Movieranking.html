The Best Movies According to Mpho

My top 3 movies of all-time.

One Piece

This is my favourite anime.Almost a millennium ago, a mighty ancient kingdom ruled the world. Twenty ambitious kings joined forces to annihilate it, erasing any trace of its existence and establishing the World Government, which still governs the world today.

Battleship

Really cool sci-fi movie,ased on the classic Hasbro naval combat game, Battleship is the story of an international fleet of ships who come across an alien armada while on Naval war games exercise. An intense battle is fought on sea, land and air. What do the aliens want?

Drive

Super beautiful film.Really artistic,A mysterious Hollywood action film stuntman gets in trouble with gangsters when he tries to help his neighbor's husband rob a pawn shop while serving as his getaway driver.
